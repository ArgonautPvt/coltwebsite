    TOURNAMENT RULES



    Playoffs Season 3 RULEBOOK:


    1. PLAYER REQUIREMENTS:


    • 1.1: Players are not allowed to join more than 1 team.

    • 1.2: Players must commit to the time that has been set beforehand by the organizers.

    • 1.3: The act of registering for this tournament implies full acceptance of all terms and conditions, rules and regulations stated, in the


    2. TEAM REQUIREMENTS:


    • 2.1: Team captains must-have WhatsApp (Compulsory) and Discord (Secondary) installed on their mobile device.

    • 2.2: A team must consist of the following members:

      5 Main Players (Mandatory)

      2 Subs Players (Optional)


    • 2.3: Teams are not allowed to replace any registered players throughout the event without the permission of the organizers.

    • 2.4: By registering, Teams acknowledge that all usage of photos, videos, and any form of recording during the tournament have been given legal consent by the players for media and marketing purposes.*


    3. TEAM AND PLAYER NAMES:


    • 3.1: Team Names can include upper-case letters, lower-case letters, numbers 0-9, underscores, and single spaces between words only.


    4. SPONSORSHIP:


    • 4.1: Teams are allowed to acquire sponsors throughout the league. Sponsorship acquisition is unrestricted.

    • 4.2: Sponsors who fall under the following limited categories are strictly prohibited. The organizer officials can update the category list at any time. The following is a nonexclusive list of limited sponsors:

      • Gambling Websites: defined as any website that aids or abets the wagering of funds on a sport/e-sport event or allows for the wagering of funds in casino games, including poker.


      Non-"over-the-counter" drugs.


      Account sharing/skin selling websites.


      Firearms, handguns, or ammunition providers.


      Websites that display NSFW imagery or products.


      Tobacco products.*



    5. TOURNAMENT SCHEDULE:


    • 5.1: Admins will contact Team captains via WhatsApp or Discord with both Team captains of the upcoming match in the group.

    • 5.2: Teams must be in the game stage and in-game lobby at the designated time. There is a maximum 10-minute waiting period.

    • 5.3: If one team is not ready at the designated time, their opponent is awarded a default win by contacting an admin (after the 10-minute waiting period) and having him check the lobby; this may differ according to the situation.

    • 5.4: Teams may not remake matches.

    • 5.5: Delivery of the respective prizes/ winnings/ giveaways may take up to 90 Days with signed receipts.


    6. CODE OF CONDUCT:


    • 6.1: Players will conduct themselves with sportsmanship and reasonably, maintaining a friendly and polite demeanor to spectators, competitors, members of the press, tournament staff, and other participants of the event.

    • 6.2: Players will refrain from using vulgar language during the entire competition. All rules of conduct also apply to chatting through the game console.

    • 6.3: Players must compete professionally. Throwing a match, halting play without cause, or showing a flagrant lack of effort will be marked as violations of player conduct. It will result in match penalties, forfeit, and disqualification from the tournament.

    • 6.4: Teams not being self-aware about any announcements/ changes/ updates/ formal/ informal notices cannot be used as an excuse of any kind.

    • 6.5: The organizer has the right to apply penalties, disqualify and dismiss any registered player from the tournament, at their discretion, at any stage of the tournament, for any reason, with no prior warning to the players.*


    7. INGAME RULES:


    • 7.1: The game mode used is Custom Game: Draft Mode.

    • 7.2: The first team to destroy the opposing team's Base wins, or the First Team to Surrender loses

    • 7.3: If any player disconnects during a match, the game continues. The disconnected players are required to reconnect as soon as possible.

    • 7.5: In the case of a server crash/in-game bugs/technical issues, teams can appeal for a rematch. The organizer has all rights to call for the rematch also with reasons.

    • 7.6: Spamming Recalls and Emotes are allowed in Moderation. Any Trash Talking will not be entertained and punished severely by the Admins.

    • 7.7: Each team will get one disconnect (DC) pause chance upon noticing the host (must follow the official appeal method). If any team member gets disconnected in the game for unexpected reasons, one of your team members needs to type "PAUSE" in chat ASAP, and the referees will pause the game. The host will NOT pause the game unless notified with "PAUSE" in chat. Each pause chance will last 2 Minutes, Max. If the disconnected person can come back before the 3-minute mark, one team member has to notify the host on WhatsApp / Discord right away, and the host will inform both teams 10 secs before resuming the game.

    • 7.8: A REMATCH will only happen if the team provides proper Screenshots or Screen Record proof of Game Bugs or Crashes in a limited time frame of 5-7mins which also depends upon the game situation.

    • 7.9: If your team runs out of DC, pause chances, and someone gets disconnected; the game continues as normal, so make sure your team comes to the tournament ready and prepared.

    • 7.10: Typing during the game in All Chat is strictly prohibited except for asking for "PAUSE." Organizers have all DQ rights or warn the teams as per the situation

    • *7.11: Hacks, Mods, Plugins, or any3rd party apps are not allowed during the tournament match. Admins may examine a player's device if cheating is suspected. Not cooperating in this issue will be taken as an offense, and adverse actions will be taken on this.


    8. PENALTIES AND DISQUALIFICATIONS:


    • 8.1: Upon discovery of any Team Member committing any violations of the rules listed above, the officials may, without limitation of its authority under Section 6.5, issue the following penalty: Disqualify.

    9. ORGANIZERS' RIGHTS:


    • 9.1 Any organizer or admin may overrule all rules based on the situation. Admin decision is final after the appeal process.

    THESE RULES AND REGULATIONS ARE NOT FINAL AND SUBJECT TO CHANGE AT THE SOLE DISCRETION OF THE ORGANIZERS.*
